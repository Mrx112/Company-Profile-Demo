Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ depends on packaging type. Standard packaging starts at 100 units, custom packaging starts at 500 units, and pre-made products can be quoted per unit. We can tailor quantities for your needs.

How long does production take?

Typical lead times are 1-3 days for design, 7-14 business days for production, and 2-7 days for delivery. Total estimate is 2-3 weeks, depending on complexity and volume.

Do you offer design support?

Yes, we offer free initial design consultation on first orders. You get two revision cycles, print-ready files, and material recommendations.

What materials are available?

We offer cardboard duplex, kraft, ivory, corrugated single/double wall, and food-grade materials. Add finishing options such as matte/gloss lamination, spot UV, and embossing.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship across Indonesia via trusted couriers. Shipping costs are calculated on weight and destination, and tracking is provided after dispatch.

Can I get packaging samples?

Samples are available with standard shipping paid by customers. Custom sample production fees are refundable once you place a full order.
